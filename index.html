What is this?

Like the other *dle games, guess the five letter english word. Letters in a guess that are grey are nowhere in the word, letters that are yellow are in the word but not in the correct space, and letters that are green are in the correct position in the word.

With each new guess, another puzzle will be started to play simultaneously. When you correctly solve a puzzle, you will score points for how many guesses you had left out of seven total. The game is over when any word is not guessed in seven turns.
